About

Hella Jongerius studied design at the Design Academy Eindhoven in the Netherlands from 1988 to 1983. After obtaining her undergraduate degree, she quickly working on a couple of projects at Droog Design. Within a year pf graduating, she opened her own studio in Rotterdam where she shifted her focus to furniture and textile design. Many of her designs have a focal point on making opposites attract. For example, combining something technology based with something handmade. Her work is known to be very textured and chooses to work with textiles.


"Design should appeal to the humanity and the imagination of the viewer."
