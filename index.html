About

Hi! We are Pia and Gabby! We are both designers who believe in diversity in creative fields. Hence, we decided to set up this blog encouraging girls considering a career in Art & Design, to know about iconic women artists and designers who changed the way we see the world in different spheres of life. These are artists, graphic designers, industrial designers, product designers, illustrators, architects, fashion designers and so on. We want young girls to be inspired by their stories and know about their creative journeys so they can make an informed and empowered decision for themselves.
